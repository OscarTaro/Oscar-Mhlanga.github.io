About Me

I'm an Aeronautical Engineering student with a focus on avionics, robotics, and embedded systems. My work sits at the intersection of flight systems, autonomous control, and hardware implementation. I approach engineering problems through first-principles thinking and practical experimentation.

My technical interests include avionics for UAVs, robotic systems (particularly hexapods and self-balancing platforms), and RF systems including DIY radar and radio communications. I believe in building systems that are not only functional but also demonstrate clear engineering principles and robust design.

Currently pursuing my degree while working on practical projects that apply control theory, embedded programming, and mechanical design to solve real-world problems. My goal is to develop systems that are reliable, well-documented, and demonstrate clear engineering insight.
